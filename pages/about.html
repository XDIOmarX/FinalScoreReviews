About Us

Passionate gamers writing honest reviews since 2019.

Who we are.

FinalScoreReviews was founded by a small group of lifelong gamers who were tired of sponsored content and inflated scores. We believe every player deserves a straightforward answer to a simple question: is this game worth your time and money?

Our reviewers collectively play across PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo platforms. We never accept early access in exchange for favorable coverage, and our scoring system is based on a defined rubric — not gut feel. Every final score is discussed and agreed upon by at least two editors.

We cover AAA releases, indie darlings, remasters, and everything in between. If it's modern and worth playing, we'll review it.
